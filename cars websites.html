About This Website

This website is created using pure HTML and CSS. It showcases different types of cars from around the world. The goal of this project is to learn web development fundamentals and host a real website using GitHub Pages.

Types of Cars

Sports Cars

Sports cars are designed for high speed, performance, and sharp handling. Examples include Ferrari, Lamborghini, and Porsche.

Electric Cars

Electric cars run on batteries instead of petrol or diesel. They are eco-friendly and quiet. Example: Tesla.

Luxury Cars

Luxury cars focus on comfort, premium materials, and advanced technology. Examples include BMW, Mercedes, and Audi.

Supercars

Supercars are extreme-performance vehicles with powerful engines and exotic designs. Examples: Bugatti, McLaren.

Why Cars Are Important

Cars play a major role in modern life. They help people travel faster, support businesses, and enable emergency services. With electric and AI-powered cars, the future of transportation is becoming smarter and cleaner.
